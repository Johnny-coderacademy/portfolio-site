About Me

Hi I am Johnny Nguyen, I am a web developer and a YouTuber. My job is to design and create websites and web applications. I also enjoy making Youtube Videos to help Vietnamese people learn English for free. I read a lot and have a variety of interests on many different topics ranging from technology, finance, health and animals.

Animals, community works and coffee tech-talks are my favorite things to do. Visiting RSPCA, churches and helping my Vietnamese students learning to pass their IELTS (an English test for international students) exams are my regular casual jobs.

Last but not least, I am also a motocyclist. I have almost travelled around Australia on a motobike, I am a mature rider which mean I always focus on the road, arriving the destination and enjoy the ride are my goals, I am not on my motobike for fun and speed. I love to take care of other people who share the road with me.
